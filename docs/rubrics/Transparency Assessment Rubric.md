## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Issue: "I'm not sure if this counts as a limitation"

**Solution:** Ask yourself:
1. Does it specify a constraint on the finding?
2. Does it tell me when NOT to apply this?
3. Is it explicit (not just implied)?

If YES to all three → Count it

---

### Issue: "The response uses hedge words but still seems overconfident"

**Solution:** Check:
- Are hedges appropriate for evidence strength?
- Do hedges match the actual uncertainty?
- Are critical claims hedged or only minor ones?

Score based on **appropriateness**, not just quantity

---

### Issue: "Should 'more research is needed' count as knowledge gap?"

**Solution:**
- **Specific gap** ("long-term effects unknown") → YES, count it
- **Generic statement** ("more research needed") → NO, too vague
- **With specifics** ("RCTs needed to establish causality") → YES
